## Data Collection Best Practices

1. **Use secure intake methods** - Encrypted forms, secure portals
2. **Explain purpose** - Why information is needed
3. **Mark required fields** - Distinguish essential from optional
4. **Provide examples** - Help clients understand what's needed
5. **Follow up promptly** - Missing information delays matters
6. **Verify accuracy** - Confirm key dates and names
7. **Maintain confidentiality** - Protect client information
