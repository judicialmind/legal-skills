# Litigation Hold Notice Templates

## Overview

Litigation holds (also called legal holds or preservation notices) are essential to prevent spoliation of evidence. Organizations have a duty to preserve potentially relevant information once litigation is reasonably anticipated.

## Litigation Hold Notice - Standard Template

```
CONFIDENTIAL - ATTORNEY-CLIENT PRIVILEGED
LITIGATION HOLD NOTICE

TO:         [Custodian Name]
FROM:       [Legal Department / Outside Counsel]
DATE:       [Date]
RE:         LITIGATION HOLD - [Matter Name/Description]
HOLD ID:    [Tracking Number]

IMMEDIATE ACTION REQUIRED

You have been identified as a custodian of information that may be
relevant to [pending/anticipated litigation/investigation] involving
[brief description without disclosing strategy].

You must IMMEDIATELY preserve all documents and information described
below and SUSPEND any routine destruction or deletion of such materials.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

WHAT YOU MUST PRESERVE

Preserve ALL documents and information, in any format, that relate to:

Subject Matter:
• [Topic 1]
• [Topic 2]
• [Topic 3]

Time Period: [Start Date] through the present

Relevant Parties/Entities:
• [Party 1]
• [Party 2]
• [Party 3]

"Documents and information" includes but is not limited to:

Electronic Information:
□ Emails (including attachments, sent, received, drafted, deleted)
□ Text messages and instant messages (Teams, Slack, WhatsApp)
□ Documents on your computer (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, PDF)
□ Documents in cloud storage (OneDrive, SharePoint, Google Drive)
□ Calendar entries and meeting invitations
□ Voicemail recordings
□ Social media content
□ Mobile device content
□ Personal device content (if used for company business)

Physical Documents:
□ Paper files, memos, and correspondence
□ Handwritten notes
□ Calendars and planners
□ Business cards with notes
□ Photos and recordings

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

WHAT YOU MUST DO

1. STOP any routine deletion of documents related to this matter
2. DISABLE auto-delete on email and messaging applications
3. DO NOT delete, destroy, modify, or discard relevant materials
4. PRESERVE personal devices used for company business
5. NOTIFY IT if you have relevant data in unusual locations
6. CONTACT the Legal Department before disposing of ANY materials
   covered by this hold

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

WHAT YOU MUST NOT DO

✗ Do NOT delete any potentially relevant emails or messages
✗ Do NOT overwrite or modify electronic files
✗ Do NOT discard paper documents
✗ Do NOT discuss this matter with anyone outside the company
✗ Do NOT upgrade or dispose of devices without Legal approval

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

CONSEQUENCES OF NON-COMPLIANCE

Failure to preserve relevant documents can result in:
• Serious legal consequences for the company
• Court sanctions and adverse rulings
• Disciplinary action up to and including termination

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

ACKNOWLEDGMENT REQUIRED

Please acknowledge receipt of this notice by [date] by:
□ Signing and returning this notice, OR
□ Responding to this email confirming receipt

If you have questions or concerns, contact:
[Legal Contact Name]
[Phone]
[Email]

This hold remains in effect until you receive written notice of release.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

I acknowledge receipt of this Litigation Hold Notice. I understand my

comply with this notice and contact Legal with any questions.

Signature: ___________________________ Date: _______________

Print Name: _________________________

Department: _________________________
```

## Preservation Obligations

### Trigger Events

Duty to preserve attaches when litigation is "reasonably anticipated":

**Clear Triggers:**
- Receipt of complaint or subpoena
- Preservation letter from opposing counsel
- Regulatory investigation notice
- Internal investigation launched
- Threat of litigation communicated

**Situational Triggers:**
- Serious workplace incident
- Product liability incident
- Significant contract dispute
- Employee termination (high-risk)
- Whistleblower complaint
- Data breach

```
LITIGATION HOLD REMINDER

This is a reminder that the litigation hold issued on [date] regarding
```

## Best Practices

1. **Issue promptly** - Immediately upon trigger event
2. **Be specific** - Clear description of what to preserve
3. **Track acknowledgments** - Follow up on non-responses
4. **Send reminders** - Regular intervals (quarterly minimum)
5. **Document everything** - Maintain complete hold file
6. **Coordinate with IT** - Ensure technical preservation
7. **Update as needed** - Modify scope when matter evolves
8. **Release properly** - Formal release when appropriate
