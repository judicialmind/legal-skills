## Milestone Tracking Best Practices

1. **Weekly status reviews** - Check all open matters
2. **Automated reminders** - 30/14/7 day warnings
3. **Responsibility assignment** - Clear ownership per task
4. **Client reporting** - Regular milestone updates
5. **Matter close-out** - Checklist for completed matters
6. **Template customization** - Adapt to specific matters
7. **Dependency mapping** - Note sequential requirements
