# Regulatory Filing Calendar Templates

## Overview

Regulatory compliance requires meticulous tracking of filing deadlines across multiple agencies and jurisdictions. This guide provides templates and best practices for managing compliance calendars.

## Annual Filing Calendar Template

### SEC Filings (Public Companies)

| Filing | Deadline | Trigger/Period |
|--------|----------|----------------|
| Form 10-K | 60 days (LAF), 75 days (AF), 90 days (NAF) | Fiscal year end |
| Form 10-Q | 40 days (LAF/AF), 45 days (NAF) | Quarter end |
| Form 8-K | 4 business days | Material events |
| Proxy Statement | 120 days before annual meeting | Annual meeting |
| Schedule 13D/G | 10 days / 45 days | 5% ownership threshold |
| Form 3 | Within 10 days | Officer/director appointment |
| Form 4 | 2 business days | Insider transactions |
| Form 5 | 45 days after FYE | Annual insider report |

**Filer Categories:**
- LAF = Large Accelerated Filer (market cap >$700M)
- AF = Accelerated Filer (market cap $75M-$700M)
- NAF = Non-Accelerated Filer (market cap <$75M)

## Compliance Calendar Best Practices

### Calendar Management

1. **Use dedicated compliance software** or calendar system
2. **Build in buffer time** - Internal deadlines before actual deadlines
3. **Multiple reminder levels** - Different alerts at different intervals
4. **Backup assignments** - Never single point of failure
5. **Regular calendar audits** - Monthly review for accuracy

### Documentation Requirements

1. **Filing confirmation** - Screenshot or confirmation number
2. **Approval chain** - Who reviewed and approved
3. **Supporting documents** - Retain with filing record
4. **Version control** - Track drafts and final
5. **Amendment tracking** - Note any corrections filed

### Regulatory Monitoring

1. **Subscribe to agency updates** - Federal Register, state bulletins
2. **Industry association alerts** - Trade group notifications
3. **Vendor updates** - Compliance software alerts
4. **Legal counsel updates** - Firm memos and alerts
5. **Quarterly regulation review** - Comprehensive check for changes
