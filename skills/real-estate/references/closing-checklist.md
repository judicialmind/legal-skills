# Real Estate Closing Checklist

## Overview

This checklist covers the essential items and procedures for commercial real estate closings, including acquisitions, dispositions, and refinancings. The checklist should be customized based on transaction type, property characteristics, and jurisdictional requirements.

---

## 1. Pre-Closing Due Diligence

### 1.1 Title Review
- [ ] Obtain preliminary title report/commitment
- [ ] Review all Schedule B-I requirements
- [ ] Review all Schedule B-II exceptions
- [ ] Order copies of all exception documents
- [ ] Analyze each exception for acceptability
- [ ] Prepare list of objectionable exceptions
- [ ] Send title objection letter to seller (within contract period)
- [ ] Review seller's response to title objections
- [ ] Negotiate curative actions
- [ ] Confirm deletion of standard exceptions (if applicable)
- [ ] Review endorsements to be issued
- [ ] Confirm extended coverage available
- [ ] Review and approve pro forma title policy
- [ ] Confirm title company wire instructions

### 1.2 Survey Review
- [ ] Order ALTA/NSPS survey
- [ ] Verify survey meets Table A requirements specified in contract
- [ ] Review legal description matches title
- [ ] Verify boundary lines and dimensions
- [ ] Identify and review all easements shown
- [ ] Review setback lines and building restriction lines
- [ ] Identify any encroachments (buildings, fences, improvements)
- [ ] Verify no encroachments from/to adjacent properties
- [ ] Confirm access to public roads
- [ ] Review utility easement locations
- [ ] Identify flood zone determination
- [ ] Compare survey to title exceptions
- [ ] Prepare survey objection letter if needed
- [ ] Obtain surveyor's certification for lender (if financed)

### 1.3 Environmental Review
- [ ] Order Phase I Environmental Site Assessment
- [ ] Review Phase I for Recognized Environmental Conditions (RECs)
- [ ] Order Phase II if RECs identified
- [ ] Review environmental database search results
- [ ] Obtain historical aerial photographs and fire insurance maps
- [ ] Review regulatory agency files (if needed)
- [ ] Confirm no underground storage tanks or verify compliance
- [ ] Review asbestos, lead paint, mold assessments (if applicable)
- [ ] Obtain environmental insurance quotes (if needed)
- [ ] Negotiate environmental indemnities with seller
- [ ] Confirm Phase I reliance letter for lender

### 1.4 Property Condition
- [ ] Order Property Condition Assessment (PCA)
- [ ] Review immediate repair requirements
- [ ] Review capital expenditure projections
- [ ] Assess deferred maintenance items
- [ ] Review ADA compliance status
- [ ] Review fire/life safety compliance
- [ ] Obtain engineering reports (structural, MEP)
- [ ] Review roof inspection and warranty status
- [ ] Assess parking lot/pavement condition
- [ ] Review elevator inspection certificates
- [ ] Negotiate repair credits or escrows

### 1.5 Zoning and Entitlements
- [ ] Obtain zoning report or letter
- [ ] Confirm permitted uses
- [ ] Verify property is legally conforming or grandfathered
- [ ] Review applicable variances and special permits
- [ ] Confirm parking requirements met
- [ ] Review sign ordinance compliance
- [ ] Obtain copies of all building permits (issued and final)
- [ ] Obtain certificates of occupancy
- [ ] Review development agreements (if any)
- [ ] Confirm impact fees paid or calculate amount due

## 2. Lease and Tenant Matters

### 2.1 Lease Review
- [ ] Obtain copies of all leases and amendments
- [ ] Prepare lease abstract for each tenant
- [ ] Review base rent and rent escalations
- [ ] Review expense reimbursement provisions (CAM, taxes, insurance)
- [ ] Identify renewal options and expansion rights
- [ ] Review termination rights and conditions
- [ ] Identify purchase options or rights of first refusal
- [ ] Review assignment and subletting provisions
- [ ] Identify exclusive use provisions
- [ ] Review landlord work obligations
- [ ] Calculate outstanding tenant improvement allowances
- [ ] Identify lease guarantors
- [ ] Review co-tenancy provisions
- [ ] Review parking allocations
- [ ] Review signage rights

### 2.2 Tenant Estoppel Certificates
- [ ] Prepare tenant estoppel certificate forms
- [ ] Identify estoppel delivery requirements per contract
- [ ] Send estoppel requests to all tenants
- [ ] Track estoppel receipt and follow up
- [ ] Review received estoppels against lease terms
- [ ] Identify any discrepancies
- [ ] Negotiate resolution of estoppel issues
- [ ] Prepare seller estoppel (if tenant estoppels not obtained)
- [ ] Confirm estoppel requirements satisfied for lender

### 2.3 Security Deposits
- [ ] Obtain schedule of security deposits
- [ ] Verify security deposit amounts against leases
- [ ] Identify any security deposits held as letters of credit
- [ ] Arrange for transfer of letters of credit
- [ ] Calculate interest due on security deposits (if required by law)
- [ ] Prepare security deposit transfer letter to tenants
- [ ] Confirm security deposit credit at closing

### 2.4 Rent Roll Verification
- [ ] Obtain current rent roll
- [ ] Verify against lease terms
- [ ] Identify any free rent periods
- [ ] Calculate rent abatements outstanding
- [ ] Review accounts receivable aging
- [ ] Identify delinquent tenants
- [ ] Review tenant correspondence for disputes

### 3.1 Loan Commitment and Approval
- [ ] Review loan commitment letter
- [ ] Identify all conditions to closing
- [ ] Negotiate commitment letter terms
- [ ] Track satisfaction of commitment conditions
- [ ] Coordinate rate lock (if applicable)
- [ ] Confirm loan sizing and advance
- [ ] Review good faith estimate/closing disclosure

### 3.2 Loan Document Review
- [ ] Review promissory note
- [ ] Review loan agreement
- [ ] Review mortgage/deed of trust
- [ ] Review assignment of leases and rents
- [ ] Review UCC financing statements
- [ ] Review environmental indemnity agreement
- [ ] Review guaranty (if applicable)
- [ ] Review borrower organizational documents requirements
- [ ] Review borrower opinion requirements
- [ ] Review insurance requirements
- [ ] Negotiate loan document terms

### 3.3 Loan Closing Requirements
- [ ] Obtain borrower organizational documents
  - [ ] Certificate of formation/incorporation
  - [ ] Operating agreement/bylaws
  - [ ] Resolutions authorizing transaction
  - [ ] Good standing certificates
  - [ ] Incumbency certificates
- [ ] Obtain borrower legal opinions
- [ ] Coordinate title insurance for lender
- [ ] Provide survey certification to lender
- [ ] Provide Phase I reliance letter
- [ ] Provide insurance certificates meeting lender requirements
- [ ] Provide rent roll certified by borrower
- [ ] Provide operating statements
- [ ] Complete lender's closing checklist

## 5. Prorations and Adjustments

### 5.1 Standard Prorations
- [ ] Real estate taxes
  - [ ] Current year taxes (paid or unpaid)
  - [ ] Prior year tax adjustments
  - [ ] Special assessments
- [ ] Rent
  - [ ] Base rent
  - [ ] Percentage rent
  - [ ] CAM charges collected
  - [ ] Tax and insurance reimbursements collected
- [ ] Security deposits (credited to buyer)
- [ ] Prepaid expenses
  - [ ] Insurance premiums (if policy assigned)
  - [ ] Service contracts (if prepaid)
- [ ] Utility charges
- [ ] Operating expenses

### 5.2 Proration Methodology
- [ ] Confirm proration date (typically closing date)
- [ ] Determine proration basis (calendar year vs. fiscal year)
- [ ] Calculate seller's share (through day before closing)
- [ ] Calculate buyer's share (closing date forward)
- [ ] Document assumptions and calculations
- [ ] Obtain agreement on proration statement

### 5.3 Post-Closing Adjustments
- [ ] CAM reconciliation (year-end)
- [ ] Tax bill reconciliation (when final bill received)
- [ ] Operating expense true-up
- [ ] Percentage rent reconciliation
- [ ] Document adjustment calculation methodology
- [ ] Set deadline for post-closing adjustments (typically 90-180 days)

### 5.4 Sample Proration Calculations

**Real Estate Tax Proration**
```
Annual Tax:                  $120,000
Daily Rate:                  $120,000 / 365 = $328.77
Closing Date:               June 15
Seller's Days (Jan 1-Jun 14): 165 days
Seller's Share:              165 x $328.77 = $54,247.05

If taxes unpaid: Buyer receives credit of $54,247.05
If taxes paid:   Seller receives credit of ($120,000 - $54,247.05) = $65,752.95
```

**Rent Proration**
```
Monthly Rent:               $50,000
Daily Rate:                 $50,000 / 30 = $1,666.67
Closing Date:               15th of month
Seller entitled to:         14 days x $1,666.67 = $23,333.38
Buyer entitled to:          16 days x $1,666.67 = $26,666.72

If rent collected by seller:  Seller credits buyer $26,666.72
If rent uncollected:          Buyer collects full month, credits seller $23,333.38
```

## 6. Insurance Requirements

### 6.1 Property Insurance
- [ ] Obtain evidence of property insurance
- [ ] Verify coverage amounts (replacement cost)
- [ ] Verify covered perils (all-risk, named perils)
- [ ] Confirm business interruption/rental income coverage
- [ ] Verify deductible amounts acceptable
- [ ] Confirm lender named as mortgagee/loss payee
- [ ] Verify coverage effective date
- [ ] Obtain insurance certificates

### 6.2 Liability Insurance
- [ ] Obtain evidence of general liability coverage
- [ ] Verify coverage limits (per occurrence and aggregate)
- [ ] Confirm additional insured status for required parties
- [ ] Verify umbrella/excess coverage
- [ ] Review policy exclusions

### 6.3 Specialized Coverages
- [ ] Flood insurance (if in flood zone)
- [ ] Earthquake insurance (if required)
- [ ] Terrorism insurance (TRIA)
- [ ] Environmental insurance (if applicable)
- [ ] Builder's risk (during construction)

### 6.4 Title Insurance
- [ ] Standard owner's policy
- [ ] Extended coverage endorsement
- [ ] Survey endorsement
- [ ] Zoning endorsement
- [ ] Access endorsement
- [ ] Contiguity endorsement (multiple parcels)
- [ ] Lender's policy (ALTA loan policy)
- [ ] Endorsements required by lender

---

## 7. Escrow Matters

### 7.1 Earnest Money
- [ ] Verify earnest money deposited per contract
- [ ] Obtain verification from escrow agent
- [ ] Apply earnest money to purchase price
- [ ] Account for interest earned (if applicable)

### 7.2 Closing Escrow
- [ ] Coordinate with escrow agent on timing
- [ ] Deliver signed documents to escrow
- [ ] Wire purchase funds to escrow
- [ ] Confirm receipt of all funds
- [ ] Authorize disbursement
- [ ] Confirm recording instructions
- [ ] Obtain recording confirmation

### 7.3 Post-Closing Escrows
- [ ] Repair escrow (for identified repairs)
- [ ] Tenant improvement escrow
- [ ] Lease-up escrow (for vacant space)
- [ ] Tax escrow (for unpaid taxes)
- [ ] Document escrow release conditions

---

## 8. Closing Day Procedures

### 8.1 Pre-Closing Verification
- [ ] Final walk-through inspection
- [ ] Verify tenant possession status
- [ ] Confirm no new title matters (gap search)
- [ ] Verify seller authority documents current
- [ ] Confirm insurance in place
- [ ] Verify funds availability
- [ ] Confirm wire instructions

### 8.2 Closing Meeting
- [ ] Execute all documents
- [ ] Notarize documents requiring notarization
- [ ] Exchange documents
- [ ] Fund transaction
- [ ] Authorize recording
- [ ] Obtain keys and access devices
- [ ] Obtain original documents and records

### 8.3 Post-Recording
- [ ] Confirm recording of deed
- [ ] Confirm recording of mortgage/deed of trust
- [ ] Obtain recorded documents
- [ ] Distribute recorded documents to parties
- [ ] Issue title policy
- [ ] Distribute closing package

---

## 9. Post-Closing Matters

### 9.1 Notifications
- [ ] Send tenant notification letters
- [ ] Send vendor notification letters
- [ ] Notify utility companies
- [ ] Notify property manager
- [ ] File transfer tax returns
- [ ] Update property insurance named insured

### 9.2 Record Keeping
- [ ] Organize closing binder
- [ ] Store original documents securely
- [ ] Record documents in property database
- [ ] Calendar critical dates (lease expirations, options)

### 9.3 Outstanding Items
- [ ] Track post-closing adjustment deadlines
- [ ] Monitor escrow release conditions
- [ ] Follow up on outstanding estoppels
- [ ] Complete any required post-closing deliveries
- [ ] Resolve any title curative matters

---

## 10. Special Considerations

### 10.1 1031 Exchange
- [ ] Engage qualified intermediary (QI)
- [ ] Execute exchange agreement
- [ ] Coordinate timing requirements
- [ ] Direct funds to QI
- [ ] Assign purchase agreement to QI
- [ ] Comply with identification requirements

### 10.2 Assumption of Existing Financing
- [ ] Review existing loan documents
- [ ] Obtain lender consent
- [ ] Pay assumption fee
- [ ] Execute assumption agreement
- [ ] Obtain release of seller (if applicable)
- [ ] Update guarantors

### 10.3 Ground Lease Properties
- [ ] Review ground lease terms
- [ ] Obtain ground lessor estoppel
- [ ] Obtain ground lessor consent to assignment
- [ ] Verify leasehold title policy available
- [ ] Review mortgage provisions in ground lease
