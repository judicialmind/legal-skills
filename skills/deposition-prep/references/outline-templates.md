# Deposition Outline Templates

## Overview

Effective deposition outlines organize questioning to achieve strategic objectives while remaining flexible enough to pursue unexpected leads. This guide provides templates for different witness types.

## Fact Witness Outline Template

### Standard Structure

```
DEPOSITION OUTLINE: FACT WITNESS

WITNESS: _______________________
DATE: _________________________
MATTER: _______________________
EXAMINING ATTORNEY: ____________

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

I. INTRODUCTION AND BACKGROUND
   Time Estimate: 15-20 minutes

   A. Witness Background
      1. Full name and spelling
      2. Current address
      3. Educational background
      4. Employment history (relevant period)
      5. Relationship to parties

   B. Preparation for Deposition
      1. Documents reviewed
      2. Persons spoken with
      3. Prior testimony (if any)

   C. Understanding of Proceedings
      1. Prior deposition experience
      2. Oath and truthfulness
      3. Clarification protocol

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

II. RELATIONSHIP TO MATTER
    Time Estimate: 20-30 minutes

    A. Role in Events
       1. Position/title at relevant time
       2. Responsibilities
       3. Reporting structure
       4. Key colleagues

    B. Knowledge Base
       1. How witness learned of [events]
       2. Direct observations vs. hearsay
       3. Documents created/received

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

III. CHRONOLOGICAL EVENTS
     Time Estimate: 60-90 minutes

     A. Pre-Incident Period
        1. Background context
        2. Prior communications
        3. Pre-existing relationships
        Key Exhibits: [List]

     B. The [Core Event/Incident]
        1. What happened (step by step)
        2. Who was present
        3. What was said
        4. What was done
        5. Timing and sequence
        Key Exhibits: [List]

     C. Post-Incident Period
        1. Immediate aftermath
        2. Subsequent communications
        3. Actions taken
        Key Exhibits: [List]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

IV. DOCUMENTS AND COMMUNICATIONS
    Time Estimate: 30-45 minutes

    A. Document Review
       [List specific exhibits with objectives]

       Exhibit 1: [Description]
       - Authenticate
       - Establish witness involvement
       - Question about [specific content]

       Exhibit 2: [Description]
       - Purpose of document
       - Recipients and distribution
       - Follow-up actions

    B. Communications
       1. Emails with [party]
       2. Phone calls (frequency, participants)
       3. Meetings (dates, attendees, topics)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

V. DAMAGES/IMPACT
   Time Estimate: 15-30 minutes

   A. Knowledge of Harm
      1. What witness observed
      2. What witness was told
      3. Impact on witness

   B. Quantification
      1. Specific amounts (if known)
      2. Calculations witness performed
      3. Documents reflecting damages

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

VI. CLEAN-UP AND CONCLUSION
    Time Estimate: 10-15 minutes

    A. Other Witnesses
       1. Others with knowledge
       2. Documents not yet produced
       3. Additional sources of information

    B. Clarifications
       1. Anything to add
       2. Corrections to testimony
       3. Reservation of rights (if adjourning)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

NOTES:
- Total estimated time: 3-4 hours
- Adjust based on witness cooperation
- Build in breaks (2+ hours requires break)
```

## Corporate Representative (30(b)(6)) Outline

```
DEPOSITION OUTLINE: RULE 30(b)(6) WITNESS

DEPONENT: _________________ [Company Name]
DESIGNATED REPRESENTATIVE: _________________
TOPICS NOTICED: See Exhibit A
DATE: _________________

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PRELIMINARY MATTERS

A. Representative's Qualifications
   1. Current position and tenure
   2. Departments/areas of responsibility
   3. Education and experience relevant to topics

B. Preparation to Testify
   1. Notice topics reviewed
   2. Documents reviewed (request list)
   3. Persons consulted
   4. Time spent preparing

C. Scope of Testimony
   1. Understanding of representative obligation
   2. Testifying on behalf of company
   3. Knowledge limitations (if any)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TOPIC 1: [AS NOTICED]

Objectives:
- [Specific goal 1]
- [Specific goal 2]

Questions:
1. Describe the company's [policy/procedure/practice]
2. When was this [policy/procedure] implemented?
3. Who is responsible for [area]?
4. How is this [policy/procedure] documented?
5. [Document-specific questions]

Key Exhibits:
- Exhibit __: [Description]
- Exhibit __: [Description]

Follow-up Areas:
- [Potential follow-up based on anticipated answers]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TOPIC 2: [AS NOTICED]

[Same structure as Topic 1]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TOPIC 3: [AS NOTICED]

[Same structure as Topic 1]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CORPORATE STRUCTURE AND KNOWLEDGE

A. Organizational Information
   1. Corporate structure
   2. Decision-making authority
   3. Relevant departments
   4. Reporting relationships

B. Document Retention
   1. Policies and procedures
   2. Systems and repositories
   3. Destruction practices
   4. Litigation hold compliance

C. Identification of Other Witnesses
   1. Others with relevant knowledge
   2. Former employees
   3. Third parties

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

IMPORTANT REMINDERS FOR 30(b)(6):

- Witness speaks for the COMPANY, not just personal knowledge
- Binding admissions result from testimony
- Challenge inadequate preparation
- Document knowledge gaps
- Preserve right to re-depose if unprepared
```

## Expert Witness Outline

```
DEPOSITION OUTLINE: EXPERT WITNESS

EXPERT: _________________
FIELD: _________________
RETAINED BY: _________________
DATE: _________________

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

I. QUALIFICATIONS AND BACKGROUND
   Time Estimate: 30-45 minutes

   A. Credentials
      1. Education (degrees, institutions, dates)
      2. Professional licenses/certifications
      3. Academic appointments
      4. Professional memberships

   B. Experience
      1. Current position
      2. Prior positions (relevant to opinions)
      3. Publications (books, articles, papers)
      4. Speaking engagements
      5. Research experience

   C. Expert Witness History
      1. Prior testimony (last 4 years)
      2. Percentage of plaintiff vs. defendant work
      3. Retention by this firm/attorney before
      4. Fee arrangement

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

II. RETENTION AND MATERIALS
    Time Estimate: 20-30 minutes

    A. Engagement
       1. When and how retained
       2. What was asked to do
       3. Compensation terms
       4. Time spent to date

    B. Materials Reviewed
       1. Complete list of documents
       2. Depositions reviewed
       3. Site inspections
       4. Testing performed
       5. Materials not reviewed (and why)

    C. Communications
       1. With counsel
       2. With other experts
       3. With parties

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

III. OPINIONS
     Time Estimate: 60-90 minutes

     A. Opinion 1: [State opinion from report]

        1. State the opinion precisely
        2. Basis for opinion
           - Facts relied upon
           - Methodology applied
           - Literature/standards referenced
        3. Assumptions made
        4. Data and information relied upon
        5. Alternative explanations considered
        6. Confidence level

        Challenge Areas:
        - [Weakness 1]
        - [Weakness 2]
        - [Contrary authority]

     B. Opinion 2: [State opinion from report]
        [Same structure]

     C. Opinion 3: [State opinion from report]
        [Same structure]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

IV. METHODOLOGY CHALLENGE (DAUBERT)
    Time Estimate: 30-45 minutes

    A. Scientific Validity
       1. Can methodology be tested?
       2. Has it been peer reviewed?
       3. Known or potential error rate?
       4. Standards controlling operation?
       5. General acceptance in field?

    B. Reliable Application
       1. Methodology properly applied?
       2. Sufficient facts/data?
       3. Reliable principles and methods?
       4. Properly applied to facts of case?

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

V. IMPEACHMENT AREAS
   Time Estimate: 20-30 minutes

   A. Prior Inconsistent Statements
      - Prior testimony
      - Publications
      - Statements in other cases

   B. Bias Indicators
      - Financial interest
      - Relationship to party
      - Advocacy positions

   C. Limitations
      - Areas outside expertise
      - Assumptions that may be wrong
      - Information not considered

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

EXPERT DEPOSITION TIPS:

- Request ALL documents before deposition
- Obtain complete case list (4 years)
- Review prior testimony transcripts
- Know the expert's publications
- Have competing expert literature ready
- Document everything for Daubert motion
```

## Key Questioning Techniques

### Open-Ended Questions
```
"Tell me about..."
"Describe what happened..."
"Walk me through..."
"Explain how..."
"What did you observe..."
```

### Narrowing Questions
```
"You mentioned [X]. Let me ask you specifically about..."
"Focusing on [specific topic]..."
"Let's go back to when you said..."
```

### Pinning Down Testimony
```
"Is it fair to say that..."
"So your testimony is that..."
"And that's the complete [list/explanation]?"
"Is there anything else?"
```

### Document Foundation
```
"I'm showing you what's been marked as Exhibit X."
"Do you recognize this document?"
"Did you receive/create this document?"
"What is this document?"
```
